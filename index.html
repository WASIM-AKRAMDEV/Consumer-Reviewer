What is viscose from bamboo?

You may commonly hear viscose from bamboo fabric referred to as just bamboo. When people say it's bamboo bedding, bamboo pajamas, or a bamboo t-shirt, the full formal way to say it would be viscose from bamboo bedding.

Bamboo wood is put through a series of steps and the end result is spun into a fabric officially called viscose from bamboo.
